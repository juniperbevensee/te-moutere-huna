# Te Moutere Huna — The Hidden Island

A short story-game, made with aroha for Pirihira.

A dreamed island with exact coordinates that no map will admit to. Two friends
who have to decide whether to believe. A waka unua voyage steered by Te Punga,
a base that should not exist, and Waitī of Matariki — the star of the sweet
waters — waiting eleven winters to be given back her song.

**Play it here → https://juniperbevensee.github.io/te-moutere-huna/**

One HTML file, no dependencies, no tracking. Sound starts on your first
tap/click; space or click advances the story. Two endings.

Mānawatia a Matariki.
